About Us

“We are kept from our goal, not by obstacles, but by a clear path to a lesser goal.”.

  • Invictus, the annual technical fest of Delhi Technological University (formerly DCE) is all set and ready to roar, bringing aboard all the technical societies of DTU, in this odyssey to make this fest captivating and knowledge enriched by conducting a multitude of brainstorming events with the ideology of rendering quality platform for erudite minds to perform and compete at the best of their capabilities.
  • The fest is catering for the needs of robowar maniacs to hardware geeks, from the hackathon, conventional quizzes, brainteasers to Shark Tank theme events, drone shows, fun games and many more, all covered under the umbrella of Invictus.
  • A cosmic amount of fun and some bittersweet rivalries awaits you all to make your college experience a momentous one. So switch your notifications on and stay tuned for further updates.
